Purchase a subscription to use EASe. Please purchase it by pressing the price on the top right of your screen. Other subscriptions are in the works. We are working on a more guided approach that helps with working through the program.

EASe Legacy Subscription

Benefits of Pro

Legacy allows you to use your purchased EASe modules and select the 4 default presets to go through the program. You can purchase more modules and they will remain in your possesion even if the legacy subscription lapses. You will however need a current subscription to listen to the music modules.

Terms of Service

If you choose to purchase a Pro subscription, payment will be charged to your iTunes account, and your account will be charged for renewal 24-hours prior to the end of the current period. Auto-renewal may be turned off at any time by going to your settings in the iTunes Store after purchase. Current price for Pro is USD $99.99/6 months, and may vary from country to country.

We started introducing subscriptions and moved away from upfront purchase because of the following reasons:

  • The EASe iOS app needs constant updates with changing devices, device sizes, iOS updates.
  • The modules are hosted on our server and we need to cover the server costs.
  • We want to add more features and support as many people as possible who can benefit from EASe
