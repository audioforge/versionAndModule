EASe Guided Subscription

Benefits of Guided

The EASe Guided subscription helps you by not requiring you to purchase any modules. You can activate 3 modules at any given time. Activated modules become available for selection so they can be listened to. Once a module is activated, it will be available for you until it has been listened to for 12 sessions. After 12 sessions, you can either continue listening to the module, or you deactivate it and activate another module. Modules are ranked by intensity, so you can start with a low intensity and progress through to the highest most intense modules.
