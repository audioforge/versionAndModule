EASe Guided Subscription

Benefits of Guided

The EASe Guided subscription is a simple guided version of our program. Just press play and listen to the music. After 30 minutes, the session ends and a survey pops up to gauge your progress. Two short questions need to be answered and EASe determines how to progress through the intensities. Start your next session the next day and continue seeing improvements. All the EASe music is available and will stream automatically according to the needs of the program.

Terms of Service

If you choose to purchase a Pro subscription, payment will be charged to your iTunes account, and your account will be charged for renewal 24-hours prior to the end of the current period. Auto-renewal may be turned off at any time by going to your settings in the iTunes Store after purchase. Current price for Guided is USD $49.99/3 months, and may vary from country to country.
